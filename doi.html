US Wildfire Breakdowns

US wildfires from 1992-2009 broken down by year,month,day,cause and county.
Data Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/behroozsohrabi/us-wildfire-records-6th-edition?select=data.csv
Filtering data using multiple metrics is possible. To deselect/unfilter data simply click on the same data filters.
The dashboard can also be made full screen using the button in the bottom right.
Note: County data for some states is either missing or incomplete, there will be empty space for these counties.
Alternatively the map may also zoom all the way out this is an indication that there was no county data for your filter combination.
